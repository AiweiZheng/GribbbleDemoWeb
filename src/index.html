Features

OAuth login. Browse, Comment, your likes and shots you are following. Browse players' profiles as well as their shots. View animated GIFs. Follow Players on Dribbble. Create / Edit / Delete bucket on Dribbble. Add shots your like to buckets. Share shots you want other people to see. Animation effect to make viewing experience more fun. Six different themes. More features are comming soon.
